Aarmish Integrate: Shipping & Logistics

Aarmish Integrate shipping & logistics in one of the fastest growing service provider company in the field of Logistics services. With the perception of personal, precise and prompt service. Aarmish Integrate Shipping and Logistics is providing services start from picking up cargo from loading point to deliver it at destination point. Aarmish Integrate shipping & logistics you will get solution for your all cargo shipment related queries under one roof.

History of Aarmish Integrate

Aarmish Integrate Shipping & Logistics is well-established organization and are proud to present ourselves as one of the fastest groving clearing & forwarding agents of India in both Sea and Air cargo. Aarmish Integrate Shipping & Logisticsa Part of M/s; Mishra Road Carriers Pvt. Ltd. (Mishra Group), which was establish in 1970. Started this company as a small time transport company having one truck, but this was just the beginning of a Giant Fleet. The company gets to greater heights.

We are also having good rapport with the various government agencies in the field and hence we do not face any problem in the passing of document and clearance of goods. We are also handling project consignment at Mumbai and Nhava Sheva port. We are having good team of dedicated, professional and Hardworking staff and all clearance of consignment carried out in a clinically efficient manner without any delay.We are handling import and export consignment at NHAVA SHEVA, MUMBAI PORT and AIR CARGO.

Experience the new service & difference

Further, Transport business was witnessing new concept of transportation where Door-To-Door services (Multi Model Transport) take place were all services come under one roof. To see this a new challenge, New generation directors decided in 2006 to start new venture in name of M/s: Aarmish Integrate SHIPPING & LOGISTICS which gives one roof service to there clients and also providing Door-To-Door services to customers on motto of Personal, Precise & Prompt Service, Working as a service provider Aarmish Integrate shipping & Logistics is provinding total logistics solution right from Freight Forwarding, Custom Clearance, Transportation, Man power, Warehousing, Stock Inventory & Distribution.

In this line of services M/s; Aarmish Integrate Shipping & Logistics and associates has already bagged a few big prestigious Clients. Company is having good and reputed cliental of its own, built-up by the directors from their long outstanding career in the concerned fields and contacts of many years.
